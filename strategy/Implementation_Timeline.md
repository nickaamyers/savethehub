# Implementation Timeline
## Save The Hub Campaign - January to June 2026

**Last Updated:** December 30, 2025

---

## Overview

This timeline outlines the path from campaign launch to grand re-opening under community ownership. The timeline is aggressive but achievable, driven by the December 31, 2025 Library termination deadline.

**Total Duration:** 6 months  
**Target Grand Re-Opening:** June 2026

## Critical Path Items

**Items that cannot slip without delaying entire timeline:**

1. **Capital Campaign** (Feb 2026) - $300K required for closing
2. **CDFI Loan Approval** (Feb 2026) - Necessary for acquisition
3. **Property Closing** (March 2026) - Must have facility to operate
4. **Executive Director Hired** (April 2026) - Leadership essential
5. **Staff Hiring Complete** (May 2026) - Cannot open without team

**Contingency Plans:**

- **Capital campaign shortfall:** Extend timeline 30 days, seek bridge loan
- **CDFI delays:** Have backup lenders identified, alternative structures
- **Winrock delays:** Apply public/political pressure, media engagement
- **Staffing challenges:** Hire in phases, use contractors temporarily
