Our Story

The Arkansas Regional Innovation Hub opened in 2014 with $3.5 million in federal EDA funding, serving as Central Arkansas's premier makerspace. For a decade, it provided STEAM education, supported entrepreneurs, and fostered creative community.

When the North Little Rock Public Library System closed the Hub due to financial constraints, the community rallied. Led by founding members and board leadership from St. Joseph Center of Arkansas, we're bringing the Hub backâ€”better, stronger, and truly community-owned.
