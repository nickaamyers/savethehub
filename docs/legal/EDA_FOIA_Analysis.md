## Executive Summary

The EDA FOIA response reveals **critical compliance violations** by Winrock International in executing the Innovation Hub lease-to-own agreement with North Little Rock Library.

**Key Finding:** Winrock executed the lease on January 1, 2025 **WITHOUT prior EDA approval**, despite EDA's repeated written statements that "prior approval required" for property transfers.

**Evidence:** EDA was still reviewing the lease on December 23, 2025â€”**11+ months AFTER it went into effect**.

This pattern of unauthorized actions raises serious questions about Winrock's compliance with federal grant requirements and strengthens the case for community acquisition.

## EDA's Stated Legal Requirements

Happy Rahman (EDA Regional Counsel) repeatedly stated throughout 2024-2025:

### **Prior Approval Required**

> "Friendly reminder that, as we discussed when we last spoke, we need the requested documents from both parties BEFORE we can review and approve of the property transfer/lien transfer. This requires **prior approval from EDA to stay in compliance with the grant terms**."

### **Two-Step Approval Process**

> "Once we agree on the language, then I can **conditionally approve the lease** subject to you coming back **PRIOR TO CONVEYANCE** for final approval of the conveyance and grant transfer."

### **Grant Amendment Required**

> "Consistent with the grant terms and conditions of the U.S. Economic Development Administration (EDA) Grant #08-01-04368 and the associated EDA Agreement and Mortgage recorded against the Premises, **prior to expiration of the Lease and Conveyance, the Landlord and Tenants shall seek EDA approval of a grant amendment** to approve the conveyance of the Premises and to transfer the EDA grant to the Tenant."

### **Legal Basis**

> "Based on **grant terms and conditions** of EDA Grant #08-01-04368"

**Key Point:** This is not optional guidanceâ€”it's based on the binding grant agreement and federal mortgage.

## Library Termination Implications

### **Original Plan:**
Lease-to-own would maintain Hub mission through 2031-2033, satisfying federal requirements

**Timeline:**
- Initial Term: January 1, 2025 - December 31, 2031 (7 years)
- Extension Option: Through December 31, 2033 (2 additional years)
- Purchase: $2,500,000 minus rent credits (~$326K)

### **Actual Outcome:**
Library terminating December 31, 2025 (11 months after start)

**Implications:**
- Federal obligation: Property must serve innovation/economic development through October 2035 (10 years remaining)
- Lease was supposed to satisfy federal mission continuity requirement
- Termination after 11 months means federal purpose ending prematurely
- **This is exactly what federal mortgage prohibits as "unauthorized use"**

## EDA's Conditional Approval Approach

Happy Rahman outlined required two-step process:

### **Step 1: Conditional Lease Approval**
EDA reviews and conditionally approves lease language after document submission

### **Step 2: Final Conveyance Approval**
Before property transfer, Winrock must return for final approval of:
- Property conveyance
- Grant transfer to Library
- Grant amendment

**Neither step appears to have been completed before lease went into effect.**

1. Winrock cannot be trusted to follow federal compliance requirements

4. Congressional oversight is warranted
