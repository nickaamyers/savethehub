## Ways to Support

### Become a Member
Join as a founding cooperative member and help shape the Hub's future. Members have voting rights and ownership stake.

### Volunteer
Share your skills by teaching workshops, helping with events, or supporting operations. Volunteers earn "sweat equity" credit toward membership.

### Partner With Us
Corporate partnerships, foundation support, and institutional collaborations help us expand programming and impact.

### Invest
Community investment shares provide capital for property acquisition while earning modest returns for investors.

## Express Your Interest

To get involved, please:
1. Email us at info@savethehub.org
2. Open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/sshellabarger/savethehub/issues)
3. Connect with us on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/ARInnovationHub)

Include:
- Your name and contact information
- How you'd like to be involved (membership, volunteer, partnership, investment)
- Any specific skills or interests you'd like to share
