## Join the Movement

### Ways to Support

**Become a Member**  
Founding cooperative member with voting rights and ownership stake.

**Volunteer**  
Share skills teaching workshops, helping events, supporting operations. Earn "sweat equity" credit.

**Partner**  
Corporate partnerships, foundation support, institutional collaborations.

**Invest**  
Community investment shares ($1,000-$25,000) with 3% annual dividend.

**Basic Access ($50/month):** Facility access, equipment use, class discounts

**Maker Pro ($125/month):** 24/7 access, all equipment, private storage, voting rights

**Student/Senior ($35/month):** Discounted access with verification

**Patron (from $25/month):** Support without facility access, newsletter, events
