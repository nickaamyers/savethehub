# Mission & Vision

## Vision

An Arkansas where, regardless of demographics or location, people learn new skills and generate innovation across technology, agriculture, business, and arts.

## Mission

Community-owned organization dedicated to improving lives by inspiring innovation and expanding opportunities across making, agriculture, and the arts.

## Learn • Build • Grow • Create

**For Makers:** Fabrication tools and technical expertise  
**For Artists:** Studio space and exhibition opportunities  
**For Farmers:** Urban farming education and markets  
**For Community:** Accessible space for all to learn and participate
