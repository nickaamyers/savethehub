# Governance Structure

Democratic, member-driven cooperative ensuring community ownership and accountability.

## Cooperative Structure

The Arkansas Innovation Hub operates as a multi-stakeholder cooperative, combining democratic member ownership with professional management.

### Core Principle
**One member, one vote.** Every member has equal voice in governance.

## Member Classes

**User Members:** Makers, artists, farmers, entrepreneurs using facilities
- Voting rights: 1 vote per member
- Eligibility: Active 90+ days

**Worker Members:** Staff working at the Hub
- Voting rights: 1 vote per member  
- Eligibility: Employed 6+ months

**Investor Members:** Capital investors
- Limited to 49% of board seats
- Cannot outvote user + worker members

**Patron Members:** Non-voting supporters

## Board of Directors

**Composition:** 7-11 directors
- User Directors (4-6): 3-year terms
- Worker Director (1): 2-year term
- Investor Directors (2-4): 3-year terms

**Responsibilities:**
- Strategic direction
- Budget approval
- Hire/evaluate Executive Director
- EDA compliance

## Transparency

- Quarterly financial reports
- Annual independent audit
- Open meeting minutes
- Public GitHub repository
