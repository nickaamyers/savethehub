## Educational Programs

Hands-on learning in making, agriculture, and arts for all ages.

### Youth Programming

**School Field Trips** (K-12)
- 2-3 hour visits aligned with state standards
- STEAM projects: robotics, laser cutting, 3D printing
- Arkansas LEARNS credit eligible

**After-School Programs** (Ages 8-18)  
- 8-week sessions, twice weekly
- Robotics, coding, digital fabrication, urban agriculture

**Summer Camps** (Ages 7-17)
- Week-long intensive programs
- Maker camp, robotics, young farmers, arts

**Homeschool Programs**
- Flexible scheduling for homeschool families
- Arkansas LEARNS/EFA eligible

### Adult Education

**Skills Workshops:**
- Woodworking, welding, metalworking
- Laser cutting, 3D printing, CNC routing
- Pottery, jewelry, screen printing
- Urban farming, market gardening

**Entrepreneurship:**
- Starting a maker business
- Product development
- Marketing and sales

### Community Programs

- Open Make Nights (Thursdays 6-9pm)
- Monthly Maker Markets
- Speaker Series
- Girls in STEAM
- Growing Urban Farmers Program
