Staffing Plan

Lean, efficient team structure supporting Hub operations and programming

Staffing Philosophy

The Hub employs a lean core staff supplemented by contract instructors and volunteers. This approach reduces fixed overhead while maintaining service quality through revenue-sharing compensation that aligns instructor incentives with enrollment success.

Contract Instructors

Revenue-Sharing Model: Instructors receive 40-50% of class revenue, aligning compensation with enrollment success.

  • No guaranteed minimum - instructors earn based on enrollment
  • Hub handles marketing, registration, materials procurement
  • Instructors focus on teaching quality and curriculum
  • Flexible pool of 15-20 instructors teaching 1-10 classes each per semester

Annual Budget: $149,170 for contract instruction

Volunteer Program

Sweat Equity Volunteers

Members contributing 20+ hours/month earn membership credit:

  • Shop monitors and equipment maintenance
  • Event setup and support
  • Administrative assistance
  • Marketing and communications

Guest Instructors

Community experts teach occasional workshops on volunteer basis or revenue-share for specialized topics.
