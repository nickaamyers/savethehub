Debt Coverage Ratio

The Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) measures ability to cover debt payments from operating income:

  • Year 1 DSCR: 2.44 (Net operating income $419,006 / Debt service $171,780)
  • Year 2 DSCR: 2.93
  • Year 3 DSCR: 3.51

Lender Requirement: Typically 1.25 minimum
Hub Performance: Well above minimum, demonstrating strong coverage

Repayment Strategy

Short Term (Years 1-5)

  • Make all required payments on time
  • Build 6-month operating reserve
  • Maintain debt service coverage ratio above 2.0

Medium Term (Years 6-10)

  • Begin accelerated payments on CDFI loan if cash flow permits
  • Build capital improvement reserve for major equipment
  • Complete EDA mortgage payoff (Year 10)

Long Term (Years 11-15)

  • Retire CDFI loan fully by Year 15
  • Property owned free and clear
  • Redirect debt service funds to programming expansion

Risk Mitigation

Conservative Underwriting

Financial projections use conservative revenue assumptions, building safety margin into debt coverage calculations.

Diversified Revenue

Multiple income streams reduce risk of single-source failure affecting debt service capacity.

Operating Reserve

6-month reserve provides cushion for unexpected expenses or temporary revenue shortfalls.

Board Oversight

Monthly financial reporting to board ensures early detection of issues and timely corrective action.
