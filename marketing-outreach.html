Marketing & Outreach

Building awareness and engagement across Arkansas

Marketing Strategy

Hub marketing focuses on three core audiences: (1) families with school-age children for educational programming, (2) makers/artists/farmers for membership, and (3) community stakeholders for support and partnerships.

Digital Marketing

Social Media

  • Facebook: Primary channel for events, class registration, community stories - Target: 5K followers Year 1
  • Instagram: Visual storytelling of projects, student work, maker culture - Target: 3K followers Year 1
  • YouTube: Project tutorials, facility tours, student showcases

Website & SEO

  • Comprehensive website with program catalog and online registration
  • Blog featuring project tutorials, maker stories, Hub news
  • Local SEO optimization for "makerspace Arkansas" searches

Email Marketing

  • Monthly newsletter to members and supporters
  • Program-specific emails for class registration
  • Automated welcome sequences for new members

School & Institutional Outreach

  • School District Presentations: Direct outreach to principals and curriculum coordinators
  • Teacher Workshops: Free professional development to build relationships
  • Homeschool Networks: Partnerships with co-ops and support groups
  • University Connections: Student internships, research collaborations

Community Engagement

  • Maker Faires: Annual showcase drawing 1,000+ attendees
  • Open Houses: Quarterly tours for prospective members
  • Partnership Events: Co-hosted programs with Argenta Arts District, Library, Parks & Rec
  • Media Relations: Regular press releases, feature stories in Arkansas Times, Democrat-Gazette

Budget Allocation

  • Digital advertising (Facebook, Google): $12,000
  • Print materials (brochures, signage): $8,000
  • Website hosting & maintenance: $3,000
